## Role
This is the LFM2 **hybrid backbone** â€” the 16-layer sequence model that is the brain of LFM2-Audio. It is the bare HF `transformers.Lfm2Model` (`lfm2_audio.py:14,83`), *not* `Lfm2ForCausalLM`: it consumes a pre-assembled `inputs_embeds` stream (text + audio-in + audio-out rows already scattered by modality) and returns the all-position `last_hidden_state` after a final `embedding_norm`. It carries no LM head â€” both consumers (the text head and the depthformer audio head in `model_lfm2_audio`, and the detokenizer) do their own projection against this hidden state. The hybrid design is the LFM2 signature: most layers are cheap gated **short convolutions** (`conv_L_cache=3`), interleaved with a minority of **GQA attention** layers, which is what makes LFM2 cheap to run on CPU. Because the Python class is external (`transformers`), the readable spec is the Rust port `lfm2_hf.rs`, cross-checked against the actual `config.json`.

## How it works
The backbone runs over `inputs_embeds` of shape `(1, L, 2048)` and is invoked two ways from `model_lfm2_audio`: once at prefill (`use_cache=True`, full sequence, returns hidden + a fresh `Lfm2HybridConvCache`), then once per generated token with a 1-token `in_emb` and the threaded `past_key_values` cache (`lfm2_audio.py:199-205, 264-270`). Layout config from `config.json/lfm`: `hidden_size=2048`, `num_hidden_layers=16`, `num_attention_heads=32`, `num_key_value_heads=8`, `head_dim=64` (`2048/32`), `rope_theta=1e6`, `norm_eps=1e-5`, `conv_L_cache=3`, `vocab_size=65536`, SwiGLU FFN with `block_ff_dim=12288` and `block_auto_adjust_ff_dim=true`.

**Layer schedule (hybrid).** `layer_types` is a length-16 list; positions `2,5,8,10,12,14` are `full_attention` (6 attention layers) and the remaining 10 are `conv` (short-conv layers). Each `DecoderLayer` is pre-norm with two residual adds (`lfm2_hf.rs:366-377`):
```
x = x + operator(operator_norm(x))      # operator = ShortConv OR Attention
x = x + mlp(ffn_norm(x))                 # SwiGLU FFN
```
After all 16 layers a final `embedding_norm` RMSNorm is applied (`lfm2_hf.rs:399,420`) â€” this is the only top-level norm, since this is a bare `Lfm2Model` with no `lm_head`.

**RMSNorm (normalize-in-f32, then weight-multiply, then cast).** `operator_norm`, `ffn_norm`, the per-head `q_layernorm`/`k_layernorm`, and `embedding_norm` are all RMSNorm with `eps=1e-5`. The exact op order matters at bf16 (`transformer.rs:135-141` / `lfm2_hf.rs:30`): upcast `x` to f32 â†’ `x * recip(sqrt(mean(xÂ˛, -1, keepdim) + eps))` â†’ multiply by the weight (also upcast to f32) â†’ cast back to input dtype. The weight multiply happens **in f32 before the down-cast** â€” this is the faithful LFM2/`liquid_audio` order `(_norm(x.float()) * weight).type_as(x)`, distinct from candle/moshi which cast back to the input dtype *before* the weight multiply. `recip(sqrt(Â·))` (two ops) stands in for torch's fused `rsqrt`, the ~1 ULP cross-library floor.

**Attention layer â€” GQA, qk-RMSNorm, half-split RoPE, eager SDPA-math (`lfm2_hf.rs:199-270`).** Projections are bias-free `linear_no_bias`: `q_proj` â†’ `32*64`, `k_proj`/`v_proj` â†’ `8*64` (8 KV heads, 4Ă— grouping), `out_proj` back to 2048. After reshape to `(b, heads, seq, 64)`, q and k each pass through a **per-head RMSNorm** (`q_layernorm`/`k_layernorm`, dim=head_dim=64) â€” this is the LFM2 qk-norm, applied *before* RoPE (`lfm2_hf.rs:234-237`). RoPE is **NeoX half-split** (`rope_slow`, not the interleaved `rope_i` used by the depthformer): the cos/sin tables are built from `inv_freq[i] = 1/theta^(i/head_dim)` for even `i`, `theta=1e6`, `max_position_embeddings=128000` (`lfm2_hf.rs:115-126`). KV cache concatenates new k/v along the time axis (dim 2) per layer (`lfm2_hf.rs:239-250`). GQA is realized by `repeat_kv` expanding the 8 KV heads to 32 (`n_head/n_kv = 4`, `lfm2_hf.rs:142-148, 252-253`). The score is the **eager** SDPA math, not flash-attn: upcast q,k,v to f32 â†’ `att = qÂ·káµ€ / sqrt(head_dim)` (scale `1/8`) â†’ add the additive causal mask (`-inf` above the diagonal; skipped when `seq_len==1` since a single new token attends to all of history) â†’ `softmax` over the last dim (differentiable, not the fused `softmax_last_dim`) â†’ `attÂ·v` â†’ cast back to model dtype â†’ reshape `(b, seq, 2048)` â†’ `out_proj`. This is the `sdpa`/no-flash path (HF picks `flash_attention_2` if importable, else `sdpa`, at `lfm2_audio.py:162-165`), and is exactly the path the f32 golden tensors were dumped from.

**ShortConv layer â€” gated causal depthwise conv (`lfm2_hf.rs:281-339`).** This is the LFM2 `conv_L_cache` operator. `in_proj` maps `2048 â†’ 3*2048` and splits into three streams along the channel axis: B (`bgate`), C (`c`-gate), x_proj. Compute `bx = bgate * x_proj` (elementwise gating), then a **causal depthwise conv1d** over time with `groups=hidden_size` (per-channel), kernel length `l_cache=3`, left-padding `l_cache-1=2` (`Conv1dConfig{padding:2, groups:2048}`) and the output narrowed back to `seq_len` (`lfm2_hf.rs:317-323`) to keep it causal. The result is gated again by `c`, transposed back, and passed through `out_proj` (`2048â†’2048`). **Single-step path** (`seq_len==1`, decode): instead of a Conv1d, it keeps a rolling conv state `(b, 2048, l_cache)` in the cache, shifts in the new `bx`, and computes `sum_keepdim((state * conv_weight))` â€” a gather-mul-sum equivalent of the conv. `conv_bias=false`. This is the candle reimplementation of the CUDA `causal_conv1d` kernel.

**SwiGLU FFN (`lfm2_hf.rs:164-185`).** Three bias-free linears named `w1`/`w3`/`w2`: `gate = silu(w1(x))`, `up = w3(x)`, `out = w2(gate * up)`. SiLU is the activation (LFM2 SwiGLU). The intermediate size is computed (`lfm2_hf.rs:91-100`) from `block_ff_dim=12288`: with `block_auto_adjust_ff_dim`, reduce by `2/3` â†’ `int(2*12288/3)=8192`, scale by ffn multiplier (1.0), round up to `block_multiple_of=256` â†’ **8192**. (The naive `4*hidden` form coincidentally also gives 8192 here, but the general formula is what the detokenizer's differently-sized FFN needs.)

The hidden state is the all-position tensor post-`embedding_norm`; `model_lfm2_audio` slices the **last** position for sampling (`output_embeddings[0, -1]`, `lfm2_audio.py:208,273`).

## Wiring
**Upstream:** `model_lfm2_audio._prefill` assembles `in_emb` `(1, L, 2048)` model dtype by scatter-writing three sources into one buffer by `modality_flag` and feeds it here (`lfm2_audio.py:366-372, 388, 199-203`). See [model_lfm2_audio](MD01-LFM2AudioModel) for the scatter.

**Downstream:** the `(1, L, 2048)` `last_hidden_state` feeds back into [model_lfm2_audio](MD01-LFM2AudioModel): its last row is projected by `nn.functional.linear(h, lfm.embed_tokens.weight)` â†’ text logits `(65536,)` (the **tied** text head reuses this backbone's `embed_tokens.weight`, `lfm2_audio.py:208,273`), and by `depth_linear` â†’ the [depthformer](MD04-Depthformer) audio head producing an 8-codebook audio frame. The same backbone (a separate weight set) is also the core of the [core_detokenizer](CO02-Detokenizer) ISTFT vocoder.

Deliberate divergences (PYTHON_VS_RUST.md):
- **Â§2.1 device-agnostic:** Python hard-defaults `device="cuda", dtype=bf16`; Rust takes `device/dtype` and defaults `(Cpu, F32)`. CPUâ†’f32 is the correct mapping (no candle CPU bf16 matmul). This is what makes the backbone actually run on CPU (parity passes on `Device::Cpu`).
- **Â§2.2 kernel-free:** HF's `flash_attention_2`/`sdpa` (`lfm2_audio.py:162`) â†’ eager matmul + additive causal mask + softmax (the `sdpa`/no-flash math, *not* flash-attn online-softmax). The CUDA `causal_conv1d` short-conv â†’ candle `Conv1d` (prefill) + gather-mul-sum (single step). No custom CUDA kernels â†’ byte-exact on CPU.
- **Â§2.4 RMSNorm precision order:** the port composes RMSNorm from candle tensor ops in `liquid_audio`'s order (weight-multiply in f32 *then* cast) rather than wrapping `candle_nn::RmsNorm` (which casts first); more faithful at bf16.
- RoPE/softmax use the differentiable `rope_slow`/`ops::softmax` (not fused `rope`/`softmax_last_dim`) so the trained graph keeps gradients; same forward values.

## Precision / gotchas
- **Backbone parity 6.558e-6** over 24 stacked layers (~2.7e-7/layer) is the cross-library f32 floor (candle gemm reduction order + libm transcendentals vs torch/BLAS), not a bug. The tied **text logits** parity is 5.505e-6.
- **RMSNorm bf16 order** is load-bearing: multiply the weight in f32 *before* casting down. Getting this backwards (cast-then-multiply, as candle/moshi do) diverges at bf16.
- **qk-norm is per-head and pre-RoPE** (dim=64, eps=1e-5). Applying it after RoPE or at the wrong dimensionality breaks parity.
- **Attention scale is `1/sqrt(head_dim)=1/8`**, on head_dim=64 â€” not on the full 2048 width.
- **Single-token causal shortcut:** when `seq_len==1` the additive causal mask is skipped (the new token legitimately attends to all cached history); only multi-token prefill builds the `-inf`-above-diagonal mask. The short-conv has a parallel `seq_len==1` rolling-state branch.
- **Hybrid schedule must follow `layer_types` exactly:** 6 attention layers at indices `2,5,8,10,12,14`, 10 short-conv layers elsewhere. A mismatched schedule loads wrong weights into wrong operators.
- **SwiGLU intermediate size** must come from the `2/3` + round-to-256 formula (â†’8192 here), not a hardcoded `4*hidden`; the formula is what keeps the differently-sized detokenizer FFN correct.
- This bare `Lfm2Model` has **no lm_head**; the text head is *tied* to `embed_tokens.weight`. Special tokens read off the sampled text token in `model_lfm2_audio`, not here: `128 = <|audio_start|>`, `130 = <|text_end|>`, `7 = <|im_end|>` (EOS).
